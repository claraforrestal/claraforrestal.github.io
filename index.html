About Me

Hello, my name is Clara and I’m a product designer and a front end developer based in Charlotte, NC. I currently work as a Product Design Consultant at a software consultancy that specializes in end-to-end digital transformations for various clients. I’m passionate about creating and maintaining robust design systems that both designers and developers can utilize to improve processes.

I bring a development background and creative problem-solving approach to design digital experiences. In my consulting work, I have worked in many roles depending on the client’s needs. I have developed and styled functional React and Angular components and pages. I have facilitated UX strategy workshops and user interviews in order to gather information on both the business problems and the user needs of a product. I have iteratively created low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity page designs resulting in elegant, clickable prototypes that clients love and developers utilize.

Regardless of my role on a project, I consistently prioritize the product’s users. User research should be a core part of designing and developing solutions. Research findings coupled with a complete understanding of the business problem to be solved will ultimately result in world-class products.

My Design Process

Understand the problem to be solved.

Get to know the users and their needs.

Define the Information Architecture.

Establish a Design System.

Create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups.

Continually iterate based on feedback and testing.
